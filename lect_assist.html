Lecturer's Assistant

As a lecturer's assistant in general, I have a task to help the lecturer in organizing the class. The specific tasks depend on the course. Here are some of my roles as a lecturer's assistant:

Mathematical Methods in Astronomy (Jan - Jul 2025)

  • Tutoring Astronomy students about calculus, derivatives, vectors, and series
  • Evaluate the students' coursework

Stellar Physics (Sep 2024 - Jan 2025)

  • Tutoring Astronomy students about stellar physical properties, structure, and evolution
  • Training the students in conducting computational astronomy to solve stellar physics problems
  • Preparing tests to evaluate the student's knowledge

Basic Astronomy Laboratory (Aug 2023 - Jan 2025)

  • Introducing telescopes and other instruments (such as CCD and CMOS) to Astronomy students
  • Helping the students in proposing an observation
  • Guiding the students in operating a telescope for astronomical observation
  • Tutoring the students in analyzing the observational data
